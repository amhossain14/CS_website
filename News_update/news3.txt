Warrior Hacks is a 12 hour hackathon scheduled in December 3rd, 2016 at WSU Kryzsko Commons that plans to bring together students in teams of 1-4 from across the campus to work on anything they set their mind to. There are workshops, mentors, food, swag, and buckets of coffee to guide you along the way.You bring your ideas, and we give you everything you need to make them come to life.
